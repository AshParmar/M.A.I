Introducing KairosX1

KairosX1: The Next Generation Humanoid Robot

KairosX1 is our state-of-the-art humanoid robot designed to perform a wide range of tasks with human-like dexterity and intelligence. Equipped with advanced AI and robotics technology, KairosX1 is a breakthrough in robotic engineering.

  • Height: 1.8 meters
  • Weight: 85 kg
  • Battery Life: 12 hours
  • Mobility: Full 360-degree movement
  • AI Features: Natural language processing, emotional recognition
  • Applications: Personal assistance, industrial tasks, entertainment

Introducing XiroZ01

XiroZ01: Your Child's New Playmate

XiroZ01 is a cutting-edge humanoid robot designed with a child-friendly height and an adorable appearance. Ideal for interactive play, XiroZ01 offers a fun and educational experience for kids while showcasing the latest in robotics technology.

  • Height: 1.2 meters
  • Weight:15 kg
  • Features: Voice interaction, facial recognition, playful animations
  • Battery Life: Up to 6 hours of continuous play
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth, Wi-Fi

MEET ZEMOX8

ZEMOX8 is a robotic dog designed for companionship and assistance. It's perfect for families and individuals who want a friendly, loyal, and smart pet.

  • Height: 1.5 feet
  • Weight: 10 kg
  • Battery Life: 10 hours
  • Features: Voice Commands, Fetch Game, Home Security
